# Hi, I'm David. 

![Image title](assets/media/profile/david-finster.jpg){ align=right loading=lazy }

I enjoy solving technical problems and explaining the solutions. I've made a successful career out of that talent as a programmer, a consultant, and now as a technical editor.

## Things about me

* Since Feburary 2020, I manage the Technical Communications department at [Vultr](https://www.vultr.com).
* I designed the [Vultr Docs creator program](https://www.vultr.com/docs/vultr-docs-program-guide).
* I wrote Vultr's [Style Guide](https://www.vultr.com/docs/vultr-docs-style-guide/), API [documentation](https://www.vultr.com/api/), and [scores of articles](https://www.vultr.com/docs/) in the library.
* I launched my own consulting company in 2004. it was my full-time job for nearly 16 years, until it closed at the beginning of the pandemic.

I publish my [portfolio and reading list here](https://dfinster.notion.site). When I'm not at a keyboard, I like to cook, sail, and noodle on guitar.
